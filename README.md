# Splyttr 🧾

## What is Splyttr?

Splyttr makes splitting bills effortless. Instead of manually entering every item, just photograph a receipt — Splyttr reads it, lets you assign items to people, and calculates everyone's share instantly. Splyttr uses AI to automatically categorize your purchases and generate spending insights, giving you a clearer picture of where your money goes.

---

## Features

### Core
- 📸 **Receipt OCR** — Upload a photo and Splyttr extracts all items and prices automatically using Tesseract.js
- 👥 **Flexible splitting** — Assign specific items to specific people, split items between multiple people
- 💰 **Who paid tracking** — Specify who actually paid the bill, not just who owes what
- 💾 **Split history** — All your splits saved to the cloud, accessible anytime

### AI-Powered
- 🏷️ **Auto-categorization** — Every item is automatically categorized (Food, Drinks, Clothing, etc.) when saved
- 📊 **Analytics dashboard** — Visual charts showing spending by category, monthly trends, top split partners, and spending by day of week
- 🤖 **AI Insights** — Personalized spending analysis generated by Claude ("You spend 3x more on weekends", etc.)

### Coming Soon
- 💬 Natural language splitting — just type "pizza ₹800, drinks ₹400, Rahul didn't drink"
- 🧠 Smart split suggestions based on your history
- 📝 Monthly AI spending summary

## How It Works

1. **Scan** — User uploads a receipt photo → Tesseract.js reads the text → parser extracts item names and prices
2. **Review** — User reviews extracted items, unchecks anything irrelevant
3. **Assign** — User adds people and assigns each item to whoever ordered it
4. **Who Paid** — User selects who paid the full bill
5. **Split** — App calculates each person's share, shows breakdown
6. **Save** — Split saved to MongoDB, Claude API categorizes each item in the background
7. **Analyze** — Analytics page shows charts and AI-generated spending insights
